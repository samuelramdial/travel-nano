Our Story

For new travelers and travel lovers, we look to inform our users of currency conversion, rules and regulations of different countries. We look to differentiate ourselves through our online social blogging platform to better share information centered around traveling and help establish connections between users.

We were inspired to create this due to our interest in travel content and felt that we could create a platform to inspire other travelers to share their experiences. We want this platform to eventually serve as a major hub for our users to communicate with each other and use important traveling tools like a currency converter all in one place.
